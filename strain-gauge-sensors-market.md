<p><ul><li>Metal strain gauge Sensors</li><li>Semiconductor strain gauge Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strain gauge sensors market is primarily divided into two types: metal strain gauge sensors and semiconductor strain gauge sensors. Metal strain gauges, typically made from materials like copper and constantan, offer durability and linearity for general applications. Semiconductor strain gauges leverage the piezoresistive effect, providing higher sensitivity and precision but are more temperature-sensitive and fragile. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and civil engineering, enabling accurate measurement of strain and stress.</p></p>
