<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Consumer Electronics Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) market is primarily divided into two types: Toggle MRAM and Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM (STT-MRAM). Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic tunneling junctions for data storage, providing fast read/write speeds and low power consumption, making it suitable for applications requiring quick access. STT-MRAM, on the other hand, leverages spin-polarized currents to achieve higher density and endurance, making it ideal for high-performance computing and mobile devices. Both types offer significant advantages over traditional memory technologies, enhancing performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
