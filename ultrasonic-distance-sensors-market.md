<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Distance Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic distance sensors are classified into two main market types: analog and digital. Analog ultrasonic sensors output a continuous voltage signal proportional to the measured distance, providing smooth and real-time data for applications like liquid level measurement. In contrast, digital ultrasonic sensors deliver discrete signals, often through protocols like PWM or serial communication, which simplifies integration with microcontrollers and digital systems. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and distance measurement tasks, catering to varying application requirements.</p></p>
