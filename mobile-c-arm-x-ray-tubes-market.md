<p><strong>The Mobile C-arm X-ray Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile C-arm X-ray tubes are primarily categorized into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed target that allows for consistent image quality but may have limitations in heat dissipation, restricting prolonged use. In contrast, rotating anode tubes have a rotating target, enabling better heat distribution and allowing for higher exposure times and improved image clarity. Both types are integral to various medical applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in surgical and imaging settings.</p></p>
