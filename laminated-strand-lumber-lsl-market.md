The market for LSL is categorized into two main types: structural and non-structural. The structural type is used in load-bearing applications such as beams and headers, providing reliable support in construction. Non-structural LSL, meanwhile, is utilized for applications like interior framing and furniture, where strength is essential but not necessarily load-bearing. Both types contribute to versatility in building projects.</p></p>
