<p><p>Surgery Navigation Systems can be categorized into three main types. Optical Surgical Navigation Systems utilize cameras and visual tracking technology to guide surgical instruments with high precision, often benefiting from a line-of-sight. Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Systems employ electromagnetic fields to track instruments, allowing for flexibility in movement without requiring direct visibility. The "Other" category includes hybrid systems and advanced technologies that combine elements from both optical and electromagnetic methods, enhancing the accuracy and efficacy of surgical procedures.</p></p>
