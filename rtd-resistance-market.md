<p><p>The RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) market is segmented into three types based on wiring configurations: </p><p>1. **Two-wire RTD**: Simple and cost-effective, but susceptible to measurement errors due to lead resistance, limiting accuracy.</p><p>2. **Three-wire RTD**: Offers improved accuracy by compensating for lead resistance, making it widely used in industrial applications.</p><p>3. **Four-wire RTD**: Provides the highest accuracy by eliminating lead resistance effects, ideal for laboratory and precision applications. Each type serves distinct needs in temperature measurement across various industries.</p></p>
