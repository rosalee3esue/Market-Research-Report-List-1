<p><p>The cemented carbide plate market comprises two primary types based on grain structure: coarse grain WC and fine grain WC. Coarse grain tungsten carbide (WC) plates are characterized by larger grain sizes, offering higher wear resistance and impact toughness, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, fine grain WC plates have smaller grain sizes, providing enhanced hardness and precision, ideal for intricate machining tasks and applications requiring high dimensional accuracy. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
