<p><p>The aluminum gear pump market is segmented into three main types: internal aluminum gear pumps, external aluminum gear pumps, and others. Internal aluminum gear pumps feature gears aligned within a housing, providing efficient flow and compact design, ideal for low-viscosity fluids. External aluminum gear pumps have gears positioned outside the pump casing, suitable for higher viscosity fluids and offering greater flow capacity. The "Others" category includes specialized variations and hybrid designs tailored for unique applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
