<p><p>Thin film photovoltaic modules are categorized into several types based on the materials used. Amorphous silicon (a-Si) is known for its lightweight and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. Cadmium telluride (CdTe) offers high efficiency at a lower cost, commonly used in large-scale installations. Copper indium gallium selenide (CIS/CIGS) provides excellent performance in low-light conditions and flexible designs. Other technologies include organic and dye-sensitized solar cells, expanding options for diverse energy needs and applications.</p></p>
