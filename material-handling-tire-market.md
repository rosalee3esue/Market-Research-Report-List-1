<p><strong>The Material Handling Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Tires</li><li>Cushion Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Material Handling Tire market features two primary types: pneumatic and cushion tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering better shock absorption and traction, making them suitable for uneven surfaces and outdoor applications. They are commonly used in forklifts and warehouse vehicles. Cushion tires, on the other hand, are solid rubber and designed for indoor use on smooth surfaces. They provide a stable ride and are ideal for operations in warehouses or narrow aisles, where maneuverability is essential.</p></p>
