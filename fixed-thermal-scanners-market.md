<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fixed Thermal Scanners market is segmented into three main types based on infrared technology: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners operate in the 1-3 micron range, ideal for detecting moisture and evaluating material properties. MWIR scanners, operating in the 3-5 micron range, are effective for target detection and surveillance. LWIR scanners, covering the 8-14 micron range, excel in thermal imaging and are widely used for building inspections and firefighting applications.</p></p>
