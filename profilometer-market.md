<p><strong>The Profilometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The profilometer market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact profilometers. Contact profilometers use a stylus that physically touches the surface to measure its texture and roughness, offering high precision for various materials. In contrast, non-contact profilometers employ optical techniques, such as laser or light scanning, to evaluate surface profiles without touching the material, making them ideal for delicate or soft surfaces. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and semiconductor sectors, enhancing quality control and product development.</p></p>
